Pepperoni Pizza

This Homemade Pepperoni Pizza has everything you want. A great crust, gooey cheese, and tons of pepperoni. The secret to great pepperoni flavor? Hide extra under the cheese!

Who needs delivery when you can make this beauty at home?

Ingrediants you'll need

  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • Tomato Puree

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Stretch it out
  3. Add the tomato puree evenly
  4. Sprinkle cheese
  5. Add pepperoni
  6. Cook on 180C for 25 minutes
  7. Enjoy your delicious pepperoni pizza
