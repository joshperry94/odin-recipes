Cottage Pie

This Homemade British staple is easier to make than you think; so let's get cooking.!

Goes great with red wine as an accompaniment

Ingrediants you'll need

  • Potatoes
  • Minced Beef
  • Gravy
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Cook mince.
  2. Add the vegetables and cook, stirring, for 5 mins or until heated through. Sprinkle over the gravy powder. Add the stock and stir until well combined. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 mins or until the mince mixture thickens slightly. Spoon into a 6-cup (1.5L) round ovenproof dish.
  3. Place the mashed potato in a bowl and break up with a fork until smooth. Spoon evenly over the mince mixture. Bake for 15-20 mins or until golden.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Cottage Pie
